About Us

What began as Fran’s love for flowers has blossomed into a full harvest tradition. Each fall, Fran’s Harvest brings the warmth of the season to the Mission District—apples, pumpkins, cider, and cozy treats—while still supporting our community. Ten percent of every sale continues to fund programs fighting homelessness across the Bay Area.

Come celebrate fall with us—where every sip of cider and every crisp apple is shared with kindness.
