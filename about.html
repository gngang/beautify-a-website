About Fran

Francine “Fran” Miller began Fran’s Flowers in 1995, but her love for fall traditions led to what is now Fran’s Harvest. Growing up in San Francisco, Fran always dreamed of bringing the warmth of orchards, cider mills, and pumpkin patches into the city for everyone to enjoy.

Today, Fran’s Harvest is more than just a place for flowers—it’s a seasonal celebration. From warm cider to hayrides, Fran has created a space for families, friends, and neighbors to come together and share the joy of autumn.

And just as it began, Fran’s mission still includes giving back: every sale contributes to programs fighting homelessness in the Bay Area.
