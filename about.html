About Fran

Francine “Fran” Miller founded Fran’s Flowers in 1995. Inspired by her grandmother’s garden in the Mission District of San Francisco, Fran turned her passion for flowers into a lifelong mission: to bring joy, comfort, and hope to everyone through blooms.

Over the years, Fran has grown her small shop into a beloved community hub. Her dedication not only creates beautiful arrangements but also helps those in need. Ten percent of every sale supports programs that fight homelessness in the Bay Area.

Fran believes every flower has a story—and every bouquet has the power to make someone’s day brighter.
